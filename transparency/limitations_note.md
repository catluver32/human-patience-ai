# Limitations Note

Main limitations:

- The study used scenarios rather than a live chatbot experiment.
- Attention was self-reported rather than measured through reading time or eye tracking.
- Dropout behavior was measured through intended action rather than actual observed abandonment.
- The sample was a convenience sample and may not represent all AI chatbot users.
- The chatbot platform question allowed more than one selection, so platform percentages do not add up to 100%.
- Duplicate-content responses were found, so the paper should clearly identify whether each claim uses raw 204 submissions or cleaned 181 response patterns.
