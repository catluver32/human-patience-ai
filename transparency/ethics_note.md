# Ethics and Privacy Note

This project used a scenario-based survey about AI chatbot mistakes, frustration, trust, attention, and intended behavior.

The public repository shares aggregate research materials only. Raw survey responses are not posted because they may contain timestamps, demographic combinations, duplicate submissions, and optional written responses that could create privacy concerns.

Twenty-four retained records selected "Under 18." The supplied manuscript and supporting materials do not document the applicable consent, assent, parental-permission, or institutional/school ethics determination. The relevant procedure should be added if available before the paper is submitted or distributed as completed human-subjects research.

The findings are exploratory because participants were recruited through convenience sampling rather than probability sampling. The 181 retained records are exact response-content patterns and should not be described as 181 verified unique people.
