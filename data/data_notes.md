# Data Notes

This repository shares aggregate data only.

The cleaned 181-response basis is used for the public summary tables and figures.

Raw survey data is not included publicly because it may contain timestamps, demographic combinations, duplicate or low-effort submissions, optional written responses, and possible identifying details.

The sample was a convenience sample recruited through accessible online and school/social-network channels. The findings are exploratory and should not be treated as representing all AI chatbot users.
