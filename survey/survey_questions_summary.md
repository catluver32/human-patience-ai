# Survey Questions Summary

The survey measured:

1. AI chatbot platform use
2. AI chatbot use frequency
3. Self-rated AI chatbot experience level
4. Expected factual accuracy of AI chatbot answers
5. Overall trust in AI chatbots
6. Trust reduction after a wrong answer
7. Scenario A frustration ratings
8. Scenario B frustration ratings
9. Which failure type was more frustrating overall
10. Self-reported reading attention after AI mistakes
11. Threshold for attention shift after errors
12. First action after repeated wrong answers
13. Five-item frustration scale
14. General AI reliability
15. AI consistency and predictability
16. Optional written response about what determines frustration
17. Age group
18. Student/employment status
